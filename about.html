Testing Philosophy

I believe that quality is not just about finding bugs‚Äîit's about understanding user needs, mitigating risks, and building confidence in software. My approach focuses on:

  • Risk-Based Testing: Prioritizing testing efforts where they matter most
  • User-Centric Quality: Testing from the perspective of end users
  • Continuous Improvement: Learning from each project and refining processes
  • Clear Communication: Making testing insights accessible to all stakeholders
  • Balanced Automation: Knowing when to automate and when manual testing is more valuable

How I Work

  • Collaboration: I work closely with developers and product managers to understand requirements and align on testing priorities.
  • Handling Ambiguity: When requirements are unclear, I ask clarifying questions and document assumptions to prevent testing gaps.
  • Communication: I provide clear, actionable defect reports and explain testing decisions in business terms.
  • Proactive Quality: I identify potential issues early through exploratory testing and continuous learning.
  • Mentorship: I enjoy helping junior QA engineers develop their skills and understanding of quality principles.
